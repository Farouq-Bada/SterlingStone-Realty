Project Description

The Reserve is a high-end residential development located in Orange Island, Lekki, Lagos. Designed as a contemporary mid-to-high rise comprising a Ground Floor and eight suspended floors, it delivers modern luxury within one of Lagos’ most prestigious neighborhoods.

Carefully planned to maximize unit efficiency while maintaining generous layouts, premium finishes, and luxury amenities, The Reserve stands out for its architectural distinction, strong market competitiveness, and exceptional investment value.
